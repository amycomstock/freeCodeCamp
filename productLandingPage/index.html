Process

Potholders are handmade using an old school 18 loop by 18 loop weaving loom gifted to me by my grandmother when I was little.

Material

The loops used in each potholder are made by Harrisville Designs in Harrisville, New Hampshire. They are 100% cotton.

Usage

Potholders can be used for handling hot dishes, as a coaster, trivet, wall decoration, rug mat for stuffed animals, etc. The possibilities are endless.

Pricing

Each potholder sells for $12.00. This includes shipping.
