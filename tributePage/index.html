GENERAL HOSPITAL

Soap opera that takes place in the fictional city of Port Charles in upstate New York

Achievements

  • Premiered April 1, 1963
  • Longest running American soap opera in production
  • Created by Frank & Doris Hursley
  • Listed as one of Time magazines 100 Best TV Shows of All-TIME in 2007
  • Celebrated it's 50th Anniversary on April 1, 2013
